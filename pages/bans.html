How Banning Works

Fail2ban monitors auth.log for failed SSH attempts. After 5 failures from the same IP within 10 minutes, it adds a firewall rule blocking that IP for 1 hour.

CrowdSec goes further: it uses behavior analysis (not just counting failures) and shares intelligence with a global community. If an IP attacks another CrowdSec user, your VPS knows about it before the attack even reaches you.

Both tools work together — Fail2ban catches simple brute-force, CrowdSec catches sophisticated patterns and provides preemptive blocking.
